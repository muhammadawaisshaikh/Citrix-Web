SSIP is the Safety Schemes in Procurement. We offer our clients SSIP certification add on within certification audits for OHSAS 18001 and ISO 45001.

The validity of SSIP certification is 12 months so it has to be renewed annually.

To verify ACM’s membership with SSIP, please view link: https://ssip.org.uk/members/ For more useful information on SSIP, please visit: https://ssip.org.uk/
