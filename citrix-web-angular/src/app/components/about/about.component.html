A global network of competent specialists

CITRIX is a leading UKAS-accredited certification body in the UK. With over 280 auditors internationally, we work with thousands of clients in more than 20 countries throughout their certification process up to their successful certification that will transform their business performance.

We get to understand your business on a personal level – whether you’re an SME or a multi-site corporation, we’ll work with you closely to make sure you’re where you need to be to succeed. From identifying areas for improvement to areas of best practices, our experienced auditors provide reports with valuable outcomes to help you form a plan of action.

Dedicated to delivering positive experiences

It’s important to us that we make the certification process as seamless as possible for our clients – and even enjoyable! We demystify the process and make it straightforward, keeping you informed and causing as little disruption to your day-to-day operations as we can. And the result? A smooth, fast journey to certification.

You’ll have a dedicated account coordinator who’ll work solely with you throughout your journey, so you’ll only ever be dealing with one person who truly understands your business.

We want what you want

It’s true – we want to help our clients improve their businesses so they can become more scalable, more respected, and more qualified. Ultimately, we want you to be successful. So whether you’re looking for guidance from one of our training workshops, to start the qualification process or just some general support, we’ll be here every step of the way.
